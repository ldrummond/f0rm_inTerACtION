Crowd Sourced Code

I often struggle with finding the foundational structure behind my coding projects, especially when randomness comes into play. I am drawn to randomness, and to process based work, and have been thinking about ways to ground this randomness and process in the real world. For example, how can the psuedo-random structures of nature inform code? Can the real world flocking patterns of different types of birds inform the positioning of code elements? And even better, how can I use the creative variation of people as a way to crowd-source digital variation?
